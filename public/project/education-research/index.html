Impact of Formative Assessment on Student Achievement

Sep 5, 2020

This three-year research project investigated how structured formative assessment practices impact student learning outcomes in primary and secondary education settings. The study involved 28 schools across diverse socioeconomic contexts, with data collected from both teachers and students.

Key Research Findings

  • Significant positive correlation between frequent formative assessment and improved academic performance
  • Enhanced student engagement and metacognitive awareness in classrooms with consistent feedback loops
  • Increased teacher confidence in adapting instruction based on assessment data
  • Development of a practical framework for implementing sustainable formative assessment practices

The research has been published in several peer-reviewed journals and has informed educational policy development in multiple school districts. A comprehensive resource toolkit based on the research findings has been created to help schools implement effective formative assessment strategies.
